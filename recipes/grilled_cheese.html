Crustless Grilled Cheese

Description

These crustless grilled cheese sandwiches are great to have stashed in the freezer for when the kids need a quick snack. Easy for little hands to hold, with the cheese sealed inside and no tough crusts, theyâ€™re fun for them and convenient for you. You will need a sandwich sealer for this recipe, and they are easy to find online.

List of Ingredients

  • 8 slices white sandwich bread
  • 4 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons softened butter, or as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Butter two slices of bread on one side only.
  2. Use a knife or kitchen scissors to carefully cut the crusts off the bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet. Add your favorite cheese (like cheddar or mozzarella) on top, then cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bread is golden and the cheese is melted. Slice and serve immediately.
